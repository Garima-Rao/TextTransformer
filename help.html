Welcome to TextTransformer!

This page will guide you on how to use the features of the website efficiently.

Features

  1. Remove Extra Space
    • Description:Eliminate unnecessary spaces in your text.
    • How to use:
      1. Paste or type your text in the input box.
      2. Click the "Remove Spaces" button.
      3. The output box will display the text without extra spaces.
  2. Convert to UpperCase
    • Description:Transform all text to uppercase.
    • How to use:
      1. Paste or type your text in the input box.
      2. Press the "Uppercase" button.
      3. View the result in the output area.
  3. Convert to LowerCase
    • Description:Change all text to lowercase.
    • How to use:
      1. Input your text in the text area.
      2. Click the "Lowercase" button.
      3. Your text will appear in lowercase in the output section.
  4. Remove Punctuation
    • Description:Strip all punctuation marks from your text.
    • How to use:
      1. Input your text in the text area.
      2. Select the "Remove Punctuation" button.
      3. The punctuation-free text will be displayed in the output box.
